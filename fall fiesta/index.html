Fall Fiesta:

As we bid goodbye to the rainy days, October brings to us short-lived but beautiful autumn before we solace in the winter bells. While the weather starts to get chilly and leaves start turning yellow and brown, EFFE 21 announces the launch of “FALL FIESTA”, a celebration warm like bonfire nights, sweet like the harvest fruits and exciting like the Halloween stories. The digitally organised three-day-long fiesta has in for all a rollercoaster ride of enthralling and fun activities along with delightful and irresistible rewards. This October presents the crunchy leaves, the gentle stream, the shiny conkers, the colourful trees, and a Pre-fest overflowing with breathtaking events!
